Stock Buttons

Stock buttons are the stock-keeping units (SKUs) for each admission price point. Stock buttons associate general ledger (GL) accounts and ticket types to admission price points.

Follow the steps below to create stock buttons.

Log in to Centaman

  1. From your desktop, open Remote Desktop Viewer.
  2. Enter your credentials when prompted.
  3. Click Connect.
  4. Enter your password when prompted.
  5. From the grey text box, press any key to continue.

The next screen with orange buttons is your launch pad.
