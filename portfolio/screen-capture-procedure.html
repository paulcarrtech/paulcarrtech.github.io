Capturing Screenshots with FastStone Capture

You can use FastStone Capture to capture screenshots in a variety of ways.

Before You Start

  1. Download and install the FastStone Capture toolbar (if not already installed).
  2. Open the FastStone Capture toolbar.
  3. Arrange any open program, browser, and/or dialog windows on your desktop in the manner you wish to capture.

Capturing Screenshots

Capture Screenshots in the following ways.

Capturing Active Windows

You can capture the currently active window.

  1. Left-click on a window to make it active.
  2. Left-click on the Capture Active Window button on the FastStone Capture toolbar.

The captured screenshot crops out anything outside of the active window. (See Figure 1.)
