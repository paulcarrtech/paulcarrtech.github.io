How to share notes

You can share your notes with friends, family, and coworkers.

Anyone that you share a note with can edit that note.

Sharing notes

Share a note with the Collaborator button near the bottom edge of that note:

  1. To access the Collaborator button
    • when you want to open and share a new note, click on the Take a Note... bar near the top of Google Keep.
    • Or
    • when you want to open and share an existing note, click on that note.
    • Or
    • when you want to share an existing note without opening it, hover the Cursor over that note.
  2. To open the Collaborators dialogue box, click on the Collaborator button.
  3. To search for someone to share the note with, begin typing that person's name or email address in the Person or Email to Share With field.

The Search Results drop-down menu appears as you type. The menu suggests people to share with based on what you type in the Person or Email to Share With field. These suggestions come from your Google Contacts.

  1. To select a person to share the note with, click on a suggested contact from the Search Results drop-down menu.
  2. If you want to share the note with more people, repeat steps 3–4 until you have selected everyone who you want to share with.
  3. To share the note with your selected contact(s), click Save near the bottom-right corner of the Collaborators dialogue box.

User photo(s) or icon(s) appear near the bottom edge of the note to indicate who the note is shared with. Each user will receive an email notification that you shared the note with them.

  1. To save the note, click Close near the bottom-right corner of the note.
