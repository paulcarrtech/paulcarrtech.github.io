Apera Vue Computer Setup

Connect Peripherals to the Apera Vue Computer

  1. Connect a keyboard and mouse to the PC via the USB ports shown below.

The keyboard and mouse connect to the indicated ports.

  1. Connect the computer to the internet via the Ethernet port shown below.

The Ethernet connects to the indicated port.
Other ports are reserved for cameras.

  1. Connect a Wi-Fi antenna to the Wi-Fi card via the coaxial cables shown below.

A Dell Wi-Fi antenna with coaxial cables.
The Wi-Fi antenna connects to the Wi-Fi card via coaxial cables.

  1. Connect the monitor (not supplied) via a DisplayPort cable (not supplied) to the port shown below.

The monitor connects via DisplayPort cable to the indicated port.
Never plug the monitor into the graphics card DisplayPort.

  1. Connect the power cable, as show below.

The power cable connects to the indicated port.

Test the Internet Connection

  1. Power on the Apera Vue Computer.

  2. Open Mozilla Firefox.

  3. Navigate to https://youtube.com to confirm the Internet connection.
