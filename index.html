About Me

I have been developing instructions, how-to guides, operating procedures, proposals, and other documents describing software, technologies, and services since 2012.

My Process

I pursue information accuracy with a strong attention to detail. I verify and validate details through research, hands-on testing, and discussion with subject matter experts (SMEs). I absorb that information and interpret it for technical and non-technical audiences through careful word choice.

My Portfolio

I craft well-formed, precise, and effective language to enable action, inform decisions, and maintain consistency with the products or services being described. By championing strong visual design, organization, and legibility, I ensure users can find what they seek and understand what they find.
