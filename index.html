About Me

I have been documenting software, technologies, procedures, and services since 2012.

My Process

I pursue information accuracy with a strong attention to detail: through research, hands-on testing, and discussion with subject matter experts (SMEs). I absorb that information and interpret it for technical and non-technical audiences through careful word choice.

My Work

I craft well-formed, precise, and effective language to enable action, inform decisions, and maintain consistency with the products or services I describe. By ensuring strong visual design, organization, and legibility, I help audiences find what they seek and understand what they find.
