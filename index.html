Communicating to Your Audience on Your Behalf

I become your voice to speak to your audience. How I speak to them depends on the intended effect of your message.

To perform tasks and achieve goals, your audience needs guidance:

  • instructions
  • how-to guides
  • quickstart guides

To access and recall information, your audience needs detail:

  • quick reference guides
  • specifications
  • release notes

To make informed decisions, your audience needs explanation:

  • reports
  • executive summaries
  • proposals

These are a just few examples of documentation types best suited to audience and purpose. Working closely with you, I will develop an approach that meets your specific audience and documentation needs.
